## Overview

**MalishaEdu** is a comprehensive education consultancy platform designed to guide students through their journey of studying in China. The platform provides curated information about universities, programs, scholarships, and expert guidance to help students make informed decisions about their education.

### Key Objectives
- **Democratize Access**: Make quality education in China accessible to international students
- **Comprehensive Information**: Provide detailed, searchable information about universities, courses, and scholarships
- **Expert Guidance**: Connect students with experienced consultants for personalized advice
- **Streamlined Applications**: Simplify the application process from discovery to enrollment
- **Community Building**: Foster a community through events, blog content, and student resources

### Target Audience
- International students interested in studying in China
- Education consultants and partners
- Education agents and advisors
- Chinese universities looking to recruit international talent

## Getting Started

### For New Students

#### Step 1: Visit the Platform
Navigate to **www.malishaedu.com** or open the mobile app.

#### Step 2: Explore the Catalog
- Start by browsing **Universities** to see institutions in China
- Filter **Courses** by your desired field and degree level
- Search **Scholarships** matching your profile

#### Step 3: Create Your Account
1. Click **"Get Consultation"** or **"Sign Up"**
2. Choose **"Student"** as your account type
3. Enter email and create password
4. Verify email address
5. Complete your profile

#### Step 4: Start Your Application Journey
1. Go to **My Applications** in your dashboard
2. Click **"New Application"**
3. Select university and program
4. Fill in application details
5. Upload required documents
6. Submit application

#### Step 5: Book a Consultation
1. Click **"Get Consultation"** from navigation
2. Select your topic (University Selection, Program Planning, etc.)
3. Choose available time slot
4. Confirm booking via email
5. Join video meeting at scheduled time

#### Step 6: Stay Engaged
- Register for **Events** relevant to your interests
- Read **Blog** articles for tips and guidance
- Check **Notifications** for updates
- Track application progress in **Dashboard**

## Technical Stack

### Frontend
- **Framework**: Next.js 14 (App Router)
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **State Management**: React Hooks + Context API
- **Form Handling**: React Hook Form + Zod validation
- **UI Components**: Custom components with Tailwind

### Backend
- **Runtime**: Node.js
- **API Framework**: Next.js API Routes
- **Database**: PostgreSQL
- **ORM**: Prisma
- **Authentication**: JWT tokens
- **Validation**: Zod schemas

### Database
- **Type**: PostgreSQL relational database
- **Schema**: Prisma ORM models
- **Key Tables**:
  - Users (students, partners, admins)
  - Universities, Courses, Scholarships
  - Applications, Events, Consultations
  - BlogPosts, Notifications

### Deployment
- **Hosting**: Vercel (recommended for Next.js)
- **Database**: Managed PostgreSQL
- **CDN**: Vercel Edge Network
- **CI/CD**: GitHub Actions (optional)

### Security
- **Authentication**: JWT-based auth
- **Authorization**: Role-based access control (RBAC)
- **Password**: bcrypt hashing
- **Rate Limiting**: Redis-based rate limiting
- **API Protection**: Middleware validation
- **HTTPS**: All traffic encrypted

## Common Workflows

### Workflow 1: Student Discovering and Applying to a University

**Steps:**
1. Visit home page, click "Explore Universities"
2. Use search/filters to find universities matching interests
3. Click university card to see full details
4. Browse "Related Courses" section
5. Click course to see program details
6. Click "View Details" to go back to university
7. Click "Get Free Consultation" to book expert guidance
8. Create account if not already registered
9. Start application in dashboard
10. Track application status in real-time

**Time Required**: 15-30 minutes of exploration + application filling

---

### Workflow 2: Student Searching for Scholarships

**Steps:**
1. Navigate to Scholarships page
2. Filter by:
   - Degree level (what you're pursuing)
   - Scholarship type (what you can afford)
3. Search by university or major
4. Review scholarship details
5. Check eligibility requirements
6. Book consultation to discuss strategy
7. Save scholarships to applications
8. Track application status

**Time Required**: 10-20 minutes per scholarship

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Is the platform free to use?
**A**: Yes! Browsing universities, courses, and scholarships is completely free. Initial consultations are also free. Advanced services may have fees.

### Q: How do I verify my documents?
**A**: Upload documents to your profile. Our team reviews and verifies them within 2-3 business days. You'll receive an email confirmation.

### Q: Can I apply to multiple universities at once?
**A**: Yes! Create separate applications for each university in your "My Applications" section. You can have as many applications as you want.

### Q: How long does university application review take?
**A**: Universities typically review applications within 4-8 weeks. You'll receive status updates via notifications.

### Q: Can I book multiple consultations?
**A**: Yes! You can book as many consultations as you need with different consultants. There's no limit.

### Q: Are the scholarship opportunities guaranteed?
**A**: Scholarships listed are based on university policies and subject to change. We recommend confirming directly with universities for current offers.
