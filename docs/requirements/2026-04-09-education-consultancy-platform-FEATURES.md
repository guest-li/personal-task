# Education Consultancy Platform — Features Overview

A plain-language description of what this platform does and who uses it.

---

## What Is This Platform?

An online platform that helps students find and apply to universities abroad. It connects three groups of people:

- **Students** looking to study overseas
- **Consultancy staff** who guide and support students
- **Partners** (experienced mentors) who help students with applications

The platform handles everything from browsing universities to getting accepted — all in one place.

---

## Who Uses the Platform?

### 1. Students
Young people planning to study abroad. They come to browse universities, learn about different countries, apply for scholarships, and get help with their applications.

### 2. Visitors
Anyone who lands on the website without signing up. They can explore most of the content freely before deciding to create an account.

### 3. Consultancy Staff (Counselors & Admins)
The team running the consultancy. They manage all the content on the website, respond to student inquiries, and guide students through the application process.

### 4. Partners
External mentors — often experienced professionals or alumni — who volunteer or contract to help students. They review student essays, recommend universities, and give personalized advice.

## What Can Visitors & Students Do?

### Browse Universities
- See a catalog of partner universities from around the world
- Each university card shows:
  - Photo of the campus
  - World ranking
  - Location (city, country)
  - Number of students already enrolled
  - Special badges (e.g., scholarship available)
  - **A live countdown timer** showing how much time is left to apply
  - An "Apply Now" button that disappears once the deadline passes
- Search for specific universities by name (with auto-suggestions as you type)
- Filter universities by continent, country, state/province, or city
- Filter by ranking range, deadline status, or tags

### Browse Programs & Courses
- Browse thousands of degree programs
- Each program shows: degree level, duration, language, tuition fee, intake month
- Smart filters:
  - Degree level (Bachelor, Master, PhD, MBBS, Diploma, etc.)
  - Teaching language (English, Chinese, Bilingual)
  - Intake period (e.g., September 2026, March 2026)
  - Price range sliders for tuition and accommodation
- Sort programs by:
  - "Our Top Picks" (recommended)
  - "Most Popular"
  - "Fastest Admissions"
  - "Highest Rating"
  - "Top Ranked"
  - Tuition (lowest to highest)

### Browse Scholarships
- Dedicated catalog of available scholarships
- Filter by type (government, university, merit-based, need-based, etc.)
- See eligibility, coverage (full tuition / partial / stipend), and deadlines
- Click "Apply" to go directly to the scholarship application

### Learn About Study Destinations
- Country guides for each study destination (China, UK, Italy, Australia, Denmark, etc.)
- Each guide covers:
  - Why study in that country
  - Student life
  - Visa requirements
  - Tuition costs by degree level
  - Work opportunities (during and after studies)
  - Top universities in that country

### Book a Free Consultation
- Fill out a simple form with name, contact info, qualification, and interests
- Submit and get a confirmation email
- A counselor will reach out

### Read the Blog
- Articles about studying abroad, scholarships, application tips, success stories
- Organized by categories and tags
- Search and filter to find relevant posts

### See Upcoming Events
- View a calendar of education expos, university fairs, webinars, and campus interviews
- Each event shows date, time, venue, and registration link
- Events can be in-person (in the destination country or overseas) or online

### Check the Notice Board
- Announcements from the consultancy (service updates, job postings, program launches, cultural observances)
- Sorted by date, with pinned items at the top

### View Photo Galleries
- **Authorization Gallery**: Official authorization letters from partner universities (builds trust and proves legitimacy)
- **Activity Gallery**: Photos of past events, campus visits, student arrivals, team meetings
- Click any photo to see it full-size with zoom

### Find Office Locations
- List of all physical offices (head office + branches in multiple countries)
- Each office page shows: address, phone, email, office hours, embedded map

### Contact the Team
- Contact form for questions
- Direct phone and email
- Social media links
- Office hours listed

### Frequently Asked Questions
- Common questions answered, grouped by topic
- Expandable accordion — click a question to see the answer

### Legal & Policy Pages
- Privacy Policy
- Refund Policy
- Terms & Conditions

### Newsletter Subscription
- Enter your email to get updates about new scholarships, programs, and events
- One-click unsubscribe anytime

### Switch Language
- Use the platform in **English** or **Nepali**
- Language switcher in the top menu

### Live Chat
- A chat widget in the bottom corner for instant help
- Talk to a real person during office hours

### Sticky "Apply" Buttons
- Floating buttons on the side of every page
- Click "Apply Now" or "Get Free Consultation" from anywhere, instantly

### Apply to Become a Partner
- Visitors with teaching/consulting experience can apply to become mentors
- Fill out a form describing qualifications, experience, and motivation
- The admin reviews and approves or rejects the application

## What Can Registered Students Do?

Once a visitor creates an account (with email/password or Google sign-in), they unlock extra features:

### Personal Dashboard
- See a welcome page with a summary of their activity

### Manage Profile
- Edit personal info, qualifications, interests
- Upload a profile photo

### Save Favorites
- Bookmark universities they're interested in
- Bookmark programs they want to apply for
- View and manage their saved lists

### Track Consultations
- See all consultation requests they've submitted
- View the status of each request (new / contacted / closed)

### (Coming Later) Track Applications
- Submit applications directly through the platform
- Track progress: draft → submitted → under review → accepted → visa processing → enrolled
- See which counselor and partner are helping them

### (Coming Later) Upload Documents
- Upload essays, resumes, recommendation letters, transcripts, passports
- Get written feedback from their counselor or partner

### (Coming Later) Message the Team
- Chat directly with the assigned counselor or partner
- Keep all conversations in one place

---

## What Can Partners Do?

After the admin approves a partner application, they get their own dashboard.

### View Assigned Students
- See a list of students the consultancy has assigned to them
- View each student's profile, interests, and application details

### Provide Guidance
- Write personalized recommendations for each student
- Suggest specific universities and programs that match their goals

### Review Documents
- Read student application materials — essays, resumes, recommendation letters
- Leave written feedback so students can improve their materials

### Track Activity
- See how many students they've helped and what feedback they've given

### (Coming Later) Message Students
- Chat directly with assigned students

## What Can Consultancy Staff Do?

Staff members (counselors and admins) use a control panel to run the platform.

### Admin Dashboard
- See key numbers at a glance:
  - Total students registered
  - New consultation requests
  - Partner applications awaiting review
  - Newsletter subscribers
  - Recent activity across the platform

### Manage Universities
- Add, edit, or remove universities
- Upload logos and campus photos
- Set application deadlines (the countdown timer updates automatically)
- Add tags, rankings, and location details

### Manage Programs
- Add programs under each university
- Set tuition, duration, language, intake dates
- Mark programs as popular, top-rated, or "fast admissions"

### Manage Scholarships
- Add scholarships with eligibility, coverage, and deadlines
- Link scholarships to specific universities (or list them as government scholarships)

### Manage Country Guides
- Write and edit destination guides using a rich text editor
- Separate sections for why study, visa, cost, work opportunities, etc.

### Manage Services
- List services offered (application help, visa support, interview prep, etc.)
- Add icons and descriptions

### Manage Team
- Add team member photos, names, and roles
- Reorder how they appear on the Team page

### Manage Blog Posts
- Write articles using a rich text editor
- Save as drafts or publish immediately
- Organize posts with categories and tags

### Manage Events
- Create event listings with dates, venues, and registration links
- Mark events as upcoming, ongoing, or completed
- Feature special events on the homepage

### Manage Notices
- Post announcements to the notice board
- Pin important notices to the top

### Manage FAQ
- Add and edit frequently asked questions
- Group them by category

### Manage Testimonials
- Add student success stories with photos and quotes

### Manage Galleries
- Upload authorization letters from partner universities
- Upload activity photos (events, campus visits, MOU signings)
- Organize photos into albums

### Manage Office Locations
- Add branch offices with addresses, contact info, and map coordinates

### Manage Legal Pages
- Edit the Privacy Policy, Refund Policy, Terms & Conditions

### Manage Payment Info
- List accepted payment methods (bank transfers, digital wallets)
- Display bank account details for international transfers

### Manage External Resource Links
- Add helpful links to government scholarship portals, ranking sites, and immigration resources

### Manage Newsletter Subscribers
- See who's subscribed
- Export the list for email campaigns
- Mark subscribers as active or unsubscribed

### Manage Users
- See all registered users (students, counselors, other admins, partners)
- Change someone's role
- Suspend or reactivate accounts

### Manage Consultation Requests
- View all incoming consultation requests
- Update their status (new → contacted → closed)
- Assign requests to specific counselors
- Add internal notes

### Review Partner Applications
- See all pending partner applications
- Read each applicant's qualifications and motivation
- Approve or reject with notes
- Approved applicants automatically get a partner account

### (Coming Later) CRM Pipeline
- See all student applications as a visual board (like sticky notes)
- Drag applications between stages: submitted → under review → accepted → enrolled
- Assign counselors and partners to each student

### (Coming Later) Analytics Dashboard
- Visual charts showing website traffic, conversion rates, and application statistics
- See which countries, programs, and scholarships are most popular

## Rollout Plan

The platform will be built in three stages:

### Stage 1 — Launch (MVP)
Everything above *without* the "coming later" features. This includes:
- Full public website with all content types
- Student accounts with favorites and consultation tracking
- Partner application flow and partner dashboard
- Complete admin control panel
- Live chat and multi-language support
- Galleries, events, notices, newsletter
- All the search, filter, and sort features

### Stage 2 — Student Lifecycle
- Full application tracking (submit, monitor, communicate)
- Document upload and feedback
- Internal CRM pipeline for staff to manage students
- Messaging between students, partners, and staff
- Text message notifications
- Online payment processing

### Stage 3 — Advanced Features
- AI-powered chatbot for instant answers
- Advanced analytics and reporting
- Smart scholarship matching based on student profiles
- Currency switcher for international fee display

---

## Summary

This platform brings together everything a student needs to study abroad in one place — discovery, research, consultation, application, and ongoing support. It also gives the consultancy a complete back-office tool to manage their business, their team, and their student relationships.

The final product will feel like a modern education marketplace (similar to popular study-abroad platforms) but tailored specifically to the consultancy's partner universities and services.
